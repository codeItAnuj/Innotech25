Core Roles and Focus Areas

Social Media Manager
Oversees daily scheduling, content creation, community engagement, and reporting for all social channels.
Social Media Strategist
Focuses on high-level planning, aligning social media goals with overall business objectives, and identifying target audiences and platforms.
Community Manager
Handles direct interactions, comments, messages, and conflict resolution to build and maintain an active, loyal community.
Paid Social Specialist
Manages and optimizes paid advertising campaigns (e.g., Facebook Ads, LinkedIn Ads) for maximum ROI (Return on Investment).

Essential Skills to Master

  • Content Creation (Visual & Text): Ability to write compelling copy and use basic photo/video editing tools (Canva, Adobe Express).
  • Analytics & Reporting: Proficiency in platform-native analytics (e.g., Instagram Insights) and generating meaningful reports on **Reach, Engagement, and Conversion**.
  • Platform Expertise: Deep understanding of the **algorithms and best practices** for at least 3 major platforms (e.g., TikTok, LinkedIn, Instagram).
  • Community Engagement: Skill in maintaining brand voice while interacting authentically with the audience and handling crises.
  • Strategic Thinking: Ability to connect a single tweet or post back to a larger business or marketing goal.
