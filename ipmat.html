What is IPMAT Exam?

The IPMAT (Integrated Program in Management Aptitude Test) is the entrance exam conducted by top institutions like IIM Indore and IIM Rohtak for their Integrated Program in Management (IPM). The IPM Indore is a unique program that blends management studies with a bachelor's degree, providing a comprehensive education that prepares students for the competitive world of business. The exam tests candidates on various sections, such as Quantitative Ability, Verbal Ability, and Logical Reasoning.

What is IPM Program?

The Integrated Program in Management (IPM) is a unique five-year program designed to bridge the gap between high school education and the demands of business management studies. This program, initially launched by IIM Indore, offers students an opportunity to pursue a combined BBA and MBA degree. Today, various IIMs, including IIM Rohtak, IIM Ranchi, IIM Jammu, and IIM Bodh Gaya, offer highly demanding IPM courses.

IPMAT 2026 Eligibility Criteria

Meeting the IPMAT eligibility criteria is crucial to apply for the IPMAT exam. Here are the notable eligibility requirements candidates must fulfil for the IPMAT exam 2026:

  • Educational Qualification: Candidates must have completed their 10+2 or equivalent with a minimum percentage of 60% (55% for reserved category)
  • Age Limit: Candidates should be under the age of 20 (for general category) or 22 (for SC/ST/PwD candidates) as of the exam year. However, for JIPMAT, there is no age restriction.

IPMAT 2026 Registration Process

The IPMAT registration is an online process where students must fill out their details and upload necessary documents as per the guidelines. Here are the IPMAT registration details:

  • Visit the official website.
  • Click on the "New Registration" link.
  • Enter the necessary details to create login credentials.
  • Sign in to access and fill in the application form.
  • Upload the required documents and pay the IPMAT fees.
  • Review and submit the IPMAt application form.

IPMAT 2026 Syllabus

Topics included in the IPMAT syllabus and the JIPMAT syllabkus are almost similar. For comprehensive exam preparation, candidates must know all the topics included in the QA, DILR, and Verbal Ability syllabus. Further, they can create a strategic study plan by segregating the topics into small, manageable sections.

IPMAT 2026 Admit card

All the candidates who complete the IPMAT/JIPMAT registration process and pay the exam fee can access their admit card online. The IPMAt admit card is a crucial document required on exam day. Candidates who don't carry the admit card to the IPMAT exam centre are not allowed to enter the exam centre and appear for the test. Therefore, candidates must ensure to download the IPMAT and JIPMAT admit card following their release.

IPMAT 2026 Results

Candidates can access their IPMAT result online. The result presents sectional and overall scores achieved by candidates in the exam. To access IPMAT 2026 results, candidates must visit the official website and sign in with their application No. and password.
