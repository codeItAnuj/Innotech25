Fundamental Skills & Preparation

  • Master the Basics: Focus on grammar, tone, and clarity. Good writing is the foundation of all content.
  • Learn SEO (Search Engine Optimization): Understand how to use keywords, meta tags, and structure to help content rank in search engines.
  • Build a Portfolio: Create 5-7 high-quality samples (blogs, landing pages, case studies) hosted on a simple website or platform like Medium.

Professional Development & Growth

  • Specialize: Choose a niche early on (e.g., tech, finance, health, B2B SaaS). Specialization increases your value and rates.
  • Understand Analytics: Learn to use tools like Google Analytics to see how your content performs (views, conversions, time on page).
  • Practice Editing: The best writers are great editors. Develop the skill to critique and polish your own work and the work of others.
